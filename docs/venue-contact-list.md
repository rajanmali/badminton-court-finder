## Notes on coverage and gaps

- This covers ~16 venues/locations across the major platforms identified
  (Sport Logic/intennis-style, yepbooking, council systems, YMCA, PCYC,
  Pitchbooking) — a representative cross-section for a pilot outreach batch.
- A few venues (Alpha Badminton, BadmintonWorx, ATC, Sydney Sports Club) don't
  have a published direct email — their contact pages use forms instead. These
  are still reachable, just need the form filled rather than a cold email.
- Not yet covered: Game Court, South Suburban Badminton Association, Queensland
  Badminton Centre/Orange County Badminton Club (these last two are likely
  outside Sydney metro and may have been mis-attributed earlier — worth
  re-checking before including in outreach), and any venues in the Sutherland
  Shire / Northern Beaches / South-West growth corridors not yet searched.
- **Recommended pilot batch (5 venues, mix of platform + operator types)**:
  Pro1 Badminton, The Badminton Club (Wetherill Park), NBC Badminton, Five Dock
  Leisure Centre, Sydney Olympic Park Sports Halls — covers an independent
  operator, a multi-venue operator, a council facility, and a state-government
  facility in one batch, testing response patterns across the board.

## A note on using these for outreach

These are general business contact addresses published for customer/business
enquiries — appropriate for the partnership pitch email drafted earlier (asking
about data-sharing, not a marketing blast). Worth sending individually
(personalized per venue name) rather than as a bulk CC, both for response rates
and because that's just better etiquette for this kind of ask.
